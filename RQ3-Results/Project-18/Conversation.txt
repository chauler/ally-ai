Snippet with error: <li><a href="#">Services</li>
Location of error: In the navigation menu under the header tag.
WCAG Guideline violated: 4.1.1 Parsing
Detection Algorithm: Manual inspection
Explanation of Error: The anchor tag is not properly closed. This can lead to unexpected behavior and can cause assistive technologies to have trouble understanding the structure.
Snippet with error: <button onclick="playVideo()" style="background-color: #2E7D32; color: #FFFFFF;"></button>
Location of error: In the hero section.
WCAG Guideline violated: 4.1.2 Name, Role, Value
Detection Algorithm: Manual inspection
Explanation of Error: The button lacks an accessible name. Assistive technologies use this to help users understand the purpose of the button.
Snippet with error: <video controls>...</video>
Location of error: In the video-section.
WCAG Guideline violated: 1.2.2 Captions (Prerecorded)
Detection Algorithm: Manual inspection
Explanation of Error: The video lacks captions. Captions are necessary for deaf or hard-of-hearing individuals to understand the audio content.
Snippet with error: <input type="password" id="pwd" name="pwd">
Location of error: In the contact section, within the form.
WCAG Guideline violated: 3.3.2 Labels or Instructions
Detection Algorithm: Manual inspection
Explanation of Error: The password input field lacks a corresponding label. Labels provide a description to users of what information is required.

Location of error: In the contact section, within the form.
WCAG Guideline violated: 1.3.1 Info and Relationships
Detection Algorithm: Manual inspection
Explanation of Error: The radio button lacks a label. Labels provide a description to users of what the radio button represents.
